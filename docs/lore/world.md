# World

## Purpose

Defines the setting, cosmology, and overall rules of the game world.

## Core Questions

- What is the homeworld?
- What makes this solar system different?
- Why are ancient gateways present?
- Why is space travel necessary now?

## Open Questions

- Is the tone mysterious, hopeful, hostile, or tragic?
- Is this science fantasy, grounded sci-fi, or hybrid myth-tech?
- How visible is lore to players who ignore story text?
