# Story

## Purpose

Defines the player arc, major reveals, and narrative pacing.

## Story Structure

- Opening situation
- Inciting incident
- Midgame reveal
- Planetary milestones
- Endgame truth

## Open Questions

- Is the story mostly environmental or explicitly quest-driven?
- Does the player repair, inherit, or resist the ancient system?
- Is there a final narrative state beyond mechanical completion?
