# Enchantments

## Purpose

Defines allowed enchantments, custom enchantment needs, and progression impact.

## Policy Areas

- Vanilla enchantments kept
- Vanilla enchantments removed or reworked
- Custom enchantments required
- Planetary or boss-locked enchantments

## Design Rules

- Enchantments must support build identity.
- Enchantments must not trivialize hazards.
- Utility enchantments are preferred over flat numeric inflation.

## Open Questions

- Which vanilla enchantments undermine authored progression?
- Do custom suit upgrades replace some enchantment functions?
- Should enchantments be globally available or tied to planets and factions?
