# Armor

## Purpose

Defines defensive progression, environmental protection, and suit specialization.

## Armor Design Rules

- Armor should gate survival capability as much as combat durability.
- Suit upgrades should reflect planetary mastery.
- Mobility, utility, and hazard resistance must compete with raw defense.

## Documentation Fields

- Era
- Protection role
- Hazard protection
- Movement effects
- Upgrade slots
- Unlock method

## Open Questions

- Is there one modular suit line or multiple armor branches?
